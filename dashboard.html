Reading the comparison

A physics-informed hybrid framework retains the validated bottom-up model as the emission operator while machine learning forecasts the uncertain activity drivers. Cumulative 2026–2040 methane is strongly path-dependent.

Validation

Model diagnostics

Illustrative Scope-1 (operational) net-zero pathways for Ghana's three FPSOs. Net-zero must close both vented/fugitive methane and combustion CO₂ — and combustion CO₂ dominates the ledger. Methane is the fastest, cheapest first wedge; deeper cuts need zero routine flaring, efficiency and power decarbonisation, with the residual neutralised by carbon removal.
